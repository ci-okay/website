Christensen Industries

Founded in 1991, Christensen Industries has over 30 years experience in the Aviation and Precision Sheet Metal Industry. What began as a Cable and Wire-Harness Manufactturing Operation has evolved into a full-scale Precision Metal Production Company. As a pilot and aviation enthusiast, Keith Christensen set out with the belief that he could supply superior products at a more competitive price than what was currently available in the market.

Today Christensen Industries operates out of a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. It is our policy to provide finished product that meets or exceeds all of our customers expectations. Christensen Industries is dedicated to the continual improvement of our capabilities and products, as well as providing quality, price and quick lead-times to our customers.
